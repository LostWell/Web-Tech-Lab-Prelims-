What is Equality

To understand what equality means, the first thing we need to understand would be the word 'equal'. Equal means having a like in quality, nature or status. It also means having a like for each memember of a group, class or society.(Merriam-Webster) Equality is usually perceived as sameness, identical, identity. So what is equality, it simply means 'similarity' now a group of different persons can have the same qualities, it cannot be in all respects but at least just one. Let us say they have similarity on one specific feature but it has differences on other features. An example would be that men are equal but we cannot say that they are identical. Equality simply asks one question and that "Equal in what respect?" Equality also means that every individual is given an equal oppurtunity.

Why Equality is Important

Equality makes a society fair to each member of their community. Generalizations about a particular group are not allowed and it also prevents any section of the society to dominate each other. Equality also makes you realize that there are some people who needs more support than others do in order for them to recognize or achieve their full potential.
