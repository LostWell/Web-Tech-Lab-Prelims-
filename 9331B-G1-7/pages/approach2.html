"Treat every single person equally because it helps us progress as a society."

Equality makes a society fair to each member of their community. Generalizations about a particular group are not allowed and it also prevents any section of the society to dominate each other. Equality also makes you realize that there are some people who needs more support than others do in order for them to recognize or achieve their full potential.
