Tips:

To the pet owners, here are a few tips you can follow in regards to your pets.

  • Put your dog on a leash.
  • Provide a dog house for your dog/s or an appropriate house for other pets.
  • Train your dogs or pets
  • Keep your dogs inside your house premises.
  • Make sure that they are vaccinated for rabies.*
    • only applies to animals that may have rabies.
  • When bringing your pet outside (for a walk or a stroll), make sure to clean up after your pets like dogs.
  • Always keep in mind that there are animals, like dogs, that are sensitive in hearing and has a strong sense of smell.
  • Make sure that your pets are comfortable and well taken care of.
